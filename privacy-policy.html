Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Treasure Valley Painting ("we," "us," or "our") operates treasurevalleypainting.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website and request a review or quote.

Information We Collect

When you submit a request, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, organization or association name, and details about the services you are interested in. We also automatically collect limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and advertising click identifiers (for example, Google Click ID and UTM parameters) to measure and improve our marketing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your request and the services you asked us to review
  • To prepare and deliver competitive quotes, reviews, and related recommendations
  • To operate, measure, and improve our website and advertising
  • To comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We share your information with Treasure Valley Painting and licensed specialists or service providers who help fulfill your request. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information when required by law.

Information you submit through our forms (such as name, email, and phone number) may be shared with Adam Roe Painting — the licensed local painting contractor that serves your area — so they can contact you by phone and email about your project. This sharing applies to phone calls and email only; it does not apply to text messaging.

Communications Consent

By submitting a request, you consent to be contacted by Treasure Valley Painting and its partners by phone, text message, and email, including through automated technology, regarding your request. Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying STOP to texts, clicking unsubscribe in emails, or contacting us at leads@treasurevalleypainting.com.

SMS / Text Messaging

The mobile phone number you provide for SMS text messaging will not be sold or shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in consent is collected and used solely by Treasure Valley Painting (Procure Media, LLC).

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help.

Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics and Google Ads) to understand site usage and measure advertising performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Choices & Rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at leads@treasurevalleypainting.com.
